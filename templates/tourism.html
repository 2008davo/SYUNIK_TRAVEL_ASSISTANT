City Guides

Goris — Cultural Center of Syunik

Elevation: 1,370 m  |  Distance from Yerevan: 240 km

Known for Old Goris cave dwellings, Rock Forest formations, St. Gregory Cathedral, and as the gateway to Tatev Monastery. The 19th-century grid-planned streets make it one of Armenia's most walkable cities.

Transport: Daily minibuses to Yerevan (4–5h), Sisian (45 min), Kapan (1.5h).

Sisian — The Ancient Gateway

Elevation: 1,600 m  |  Distance from Yerevan: 210 km

Home of Zorats Karer (Armenian Stonehenge), Shaki Waterfall, Vorotnavank Monastery, and the Ughtasar Petroglyphs. A major archaeological and hiking destination.

Transport: 3.5–4 h to Yerevan, 45 min to Goris.

Meghri — Armenia's Tropical Oasis

Elevation: 600 m  |  Distance from Yerevan: 376 km

Famous for pomegranates, figs, Meghri Fortress, St. Hovhannes Church, Arevik National Park, and Silk Road heritage.

Transport: 7–9 h to Yerevan, 15 min to Agarak.

Kajaran — Mining & Mountains

Elevation: 1,950 m

Home to the Zangezur Copper-Molybdenum Combine (ZCMC), Mount Kaputjugh (3,905 m), Lake Kaputan, and superb alpine hiking routes.

Transport: 6–8 h to Yerevan via Kapan.

Agarak — Southern Gate

Elevation: 600 m

Border trade hub with Iran, subtropical orchards, and direct access to Arevik National Park and the Araks River valley.

Transport: 7–9 h to Yerevan, 10 min to Meghri.

Dastakert — High-Altitude Village

Elevation: 2,050 m

Former industrial settlement with medieval cemeteries, Mount Ayri hiking routes, and local honey production in the pristine alpine environment.

Transport: 45–60 min by private taxi from Sisian.
