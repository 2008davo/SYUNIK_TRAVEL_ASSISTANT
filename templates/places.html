Monastery

Vahanavank Monastery

A medieval Armenian monastery from the 10th–11th centuries located near Kapan. It was founded by Prince Vahan and became an important spiritual center in Syunik.

📍 Near Kapan, Syunik Province

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.8/5

Monastery

Vorotnavank Monastery

A historic monastic complex from the 10th–15th centuries perched above the Vorotan Gorge between Vaghatin and Vorotan villages in Syunik.

📍 Vorotan & Vaghatin area, Syunik

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.7/5

Heritage Site

Bekh Anapat

A medieval hermitage (anapat) situated about 4 km southeast of Bekh village near Kapan. Features a historic church and monastic structures rebuilt in the 17th century.

📍 Bekh Village, Syunik Province

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.6/5

📖 City Guides

Sisian — The Ancient Gateway

Elevation: 1,600 m  |  210 km from Yerevan

History & Heritage

  • Zorats Karer (Carahunge) — "Armenian Stonehenge"
  • Sisian Church (St. Gregory of Sisavan) — 7th-century domed-cross church
  • Aghitu Memorial — 6th–7th century funerary monument
  • Vorotnavank Monastery — 10th–11th centuries

Natural Attractions

  • Shaki Waterfall — 18 m cascade
  • Ughtasar Petroglyphs — 5,000-year-old rock carvings
  • Tolors Reservoir
  • Shamb Hot Springs

Transport & Access

Minibuses to Yerevan (3.5–4h), Goris (45 min), Kapan (1.5–2h), and Jermuk (1h).

Goris — Cultural & Architectural Hub

Elevation: 1,370 m  |  240 km from Yerevan

History & Heritage

  • Old Goris (Kores) — cave dwellings
  • St. Hripsime Church — 4th century
  • St. Gregory the Illuminator Church — late 19th century
  • Amulsar Fortress

Natural Attractions

  • Rock Forest
  • Mount Didbe
  • Vararak River
  • Khndzoresk — swinging bridge & cave village

Culture & Education

  • Aksel Bakunts House-Museum
  • Goris Geological Museum
  • Goris State University
  • Goris Dramatic Theater

Transport & Access

Minibuses to Yerevan (4–5h), Kapan (1.5h), Tatev (30 min), Sisian (45 min).

Meghri — Armenia's Tropical Oasis

Elevation: 600 m  |  376 km from Yerevan

History & Heritage

  • Meghri Fortress (10th–11th centuries)
  • St. Hovhannes Mkrtich Church (17th century)
  • St. Astvatsatsin Church (17th century)
  • Shvanidzor Aqueduct (17th century)

Natural Attractions

  • Arevik National Park
  • Araks River Valley
  • Lichk Waterfalls
  • Giant Plane Tree

Transport & Access

Shared minibuses to Yerevan (7–9h), Agarak (15 min), Kapan (1.5–2h).

Kajaran — Mining & Mountain Adventure

Elevation: 1,950 m  |  356 km from Yerevan

History & Heritage

  • ZCMC — Zangezur Copper & Molybdenum Combine (est. 1952)
  • Napat Fortress
  • Baghaberd Fortress
  • Bronze Age settlements & pagan temples

Natural Attractions

  • Mount Kaputjugh (3,905 m)
  • Lake Kaputan
  • Zangezur Sanctuary
  • Voghji River

Transport & Access

Via Kapan; 6.5–8h to Yerevan. Kapan: 30–40 min; Meghri: ~1.5h.

Attractions

  • Mount Ayri hiking routes
  • Alpine meadows and wildflowers
  • Local honey tasting
  • Medieval cemetery & khachkars

Agarak — Southern Gateway

Elevation: 600 m  |  380 km from Yerevan

Attractions

  • Arevik National Park access point
  • Araks River and subtropical orchards
  • Agarak Copper-Molybdenum Combine
  • Cross-border proximity to Iran

Transport & Access

Minibuses to Yerevan (7–9h), Meghri (10 min), Kapan (1.5–2h).
